How We Operate Here

Local Presence. United Kingdom Standards.

Congolese Teams

Our country director and our teams are Congolese. Work is delivered in French and the local languages of the markets we operate in.

Independent by Design

Where we verify a supply chain, we do not trade in it. We have no stake in the finding. That is precisely why buyers accept our data.

Honest Reporting

We never overstate what we find, and we never claim capability we do not have. In compliance work, accuracy is the entire product.

Reported to UK Standards

Work delivered in the DRC is documented, verified and reported to the standard European clients, regulators and auditors expect.
