What We Do

Tender & Procurement Services for South Africa

We support South African organisations to compete effectively for government, municipal and corporate tenders with a full suite of procurement intelligence services.

Full Tender Writing

End-to-end bid writing tailored to South African government procurement requirements, scoring criteria and evaluation frameworks.

CSD & Compliance Packs

Full support with CSD registration, tax clearance, B-BBEE certificates and all documentation required for government supplier eligibility.

Collaborative Bid Writing

We work directly alongside your team, extracting the right information from your organisation to build compelling, evidence-based tender responses.

Bid Review & QA

Already written your tender? We review, score and strengthen your responses before submission to maximise your chances of winning.

Market & Opportunity Intelligence

We identify the right tenders to pursue, when to pursue them and how to position your organisation for maximum competitive advantage.

Win Strategy & Positioning

We develop the win themes, differentiators and messaging that evaluators respond to, before you write a single word of your bid.
