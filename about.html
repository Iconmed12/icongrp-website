Connected Services.

ICONGRP is an integrated services group delivering connected care, workforce and business solutions across the UK. With over 15 years of experience, we bring five specialist divisions under one trusted group.

Our Story

Built on 15 Years of Integrated Expertise

ICONGRP was founded on a simple belief: that organisations work better when their care, workforce, logistics, facilities and consulting needs are handled by a single partner who understands how those things connect.

Today we operate five specialist divisions from our base in Newport Pagnell, Milton Keynes, serving over 100 clients across the UK with more than 5,000 staff deployed across our network.
