CQC Improvement &

We work closely with care providers who need to improve their CQC rating, offering practical, hands-on support that strengthens compliance, governance and service delivery at every level.

What We Do

Structured Improvement,
Practical Results

A poor CQC rating is not just a compliance issue. It affects your reputation, your contracts, your ability to recruit and ultimately the quality of care your service users receive. We help you turn that around with a structured, realistic improvement plan backed by hands-on operational support.

Service Audits & Gap Analysis

We conduct a thorough review of your service against current CQC standards, identifying every gap between where you are and where you need to be.

Improvement Planning

We build a clear, prioritised improvement plan with realistic timelines, measurable milestones and assigned accountability at every stage.

Policy & Process Support

We review, rewrite and implement the policies and procedures your service needs to meet the Safe, Effective, Caring, Responsive and Well-Led standards.

Staff & Operational Guidance

We work directly with your management team and frontline staff, providing hands-on guidance to embed the changes that inspectors look for.

Inspection Preparation

We prepare your team for inspection through mock assessments, evidence collation support and clear briefings on what to expect and how to respond.

Ongoing Compliance Support

Once your rating improves, we offer ongoing compliance monitoring support to ensure you maintain standards and are always inspection-ready.

Our Approach

Hands-On, Not Hands-Off

Many consultants produce reports and leave. We stay alongside you through the improvement journey, providing ongoing support until the changes are embedded and your rating reflects the quality of care you actually deliver.

1
Understand the full picture
We begin with a complete audit of your service against all five CQC domains before making any recommendations.
2
Build a realistic plan
Every improvement plan is written around your specific gaps, resources and timeline, not a generic template.
3
Implement with your team
We work alongside your people, not above them, to implement changes that stick beyond the inspection.
4
Prepare for inspection
Mock inspections, evidence gathering and staff briefings ensure your team is confident and ready when the inspector arrives.
The CQC Five Domains

We assess and support improvement across all five areas inspectors evaluate.

Safe
Protection from abuse, infection, medicine errors and unsafe care
Effective
Evidence-based care that achieves positive outcomes for service users
Caring
Staff treat people with compassion, dignity and respect
Responsive
Services organised around the needs of individual service users
Well-Led
Leadership, culture, governance and continuous improvement
