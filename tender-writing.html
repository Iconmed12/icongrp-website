What We Do

Bid Support That Goes
Beyond Document Filling

Winning contracts requires strategy, clarity and a deep understanding of what commissioners expect. We deliver all three.

Full Tender Writing

We take on your entire bid from first pass to final submission, developing high-quality, scored responses that speak directly to evaluation criteria.

  • Method statement writing
  • Quality and compliance responses
  • Social value statements
  • Pricing narrative support
  • Executive summaries

Bid Review & QA

Already have responses drafted? We review, score and strengthen them before submission, identifying gaps and sharpening your competitive edge.

  • Evaluator-eye review
  • Scoring gap analysis
  • Language and compliance check
  • Formatting and structure review
  • Feedback and redraft support

Bid Strategy & Planning

We help you decide which tenders to pursue and build a strategy that positions your organisation to win before you write a single word.

  • Opportunity assessment
  • Win theme development
  • Competitor positioning
  • Bid/no-bid decision support
  • Pipeline planning

New to Tendering

If your organisation has never bid before, we guide you through the entire process from registration to submission with hands-on support at every step.

  • Procurement portal setup
  • Pre-qualification questionnaires
  • Policy and document preparation
  • Framework registration support
  • First bid hand-holding

Collaborative Bidding

Work alongside your internal team rather than handing everything over. We embed with your people and add senior bid expertise where it is needed most.

  • Collaborative writing sessions
  • SME interview and extraction
  • Internal team upskilling
  • Structured bid planning
  • Deadline and workflow management

Post-Bid Debrief

Win or lose, we help you learn. We analyse debrief feedback and build a stronger foundation for your next submission.

  • Debrief analysis
  • Scoring breakdown review
  • Improvement recommendations
  • Content library development
  • Next bid preparation
How It Works

Our Bid Process

A structured, proven approach that maximises your chances of winning from day one.

01

Discovery Call

We review the tender documents with you, understand your organisation and identify the win themes that will set your bid apart.

02

Strategy & Planning

We build a bid plan with clear ownership, deadlines and messaging strategy aligned to the evaluation criteria and scoring framework.

03

Writing & Review

Our team writes, reviews and refines each response, ensuring every answer directly addresses the question and scores as high as possible.

04

Final Submission

We complete a final compliance check, format the submission to specification and support you through the portal upload and confirmation.
